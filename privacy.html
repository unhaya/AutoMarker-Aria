AutoMarker Aria (Chrome Extension)

No data collected No external servers Runs locally

AutoMarker Aria is a browser extension that highlights your search words in distinct colors, on search engine results pages and on the pages you visit from them. It is designed to operate entirely within your browser.

What we collect

Nothing. AutoMarker Aria does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server. There is no account, no login, and no analytics.

What stays on your device

  • Your color preferences (L1–L8) and the on/off state are saved locally using Chrome's chrome.storage.local. This never leaves your browser.
  • Your most recent search words are kept locally only so the same words can be highlighted on the pages you open from the results. They are not transmitted anywhere.

Permissions and why they are needed

  • Host access to all sites (<all_urls>) — required because the extension highlights your search words on any page you visit from search results. The set of sites cannot be known in advance, so broad host access is necessary for the core feature to work. This access is used solely to read and visually highlight text on the page; page content is never sent off your device.
  • Storage — to save your color and on/off preferences locally.
  • Tabs / activeTab / scripting — to re-apply highlighting when you navigate.

No third parties

AutoMarker Aria makes no network requests of its own. No data is shared with Greenleaf Planning, Google, or any other party.
